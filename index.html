Blind Source Signal Separation of Polyphonic Music Signals

The primary objective of this project was to conduct a comprehensive evaluation and comparison of various methodologies employed in Blind Source Separation (BSS) for analyzing harmonically related polyphonic signals. The scope of the study encompasses diverse scenarios, including the extraction of distinct instrumental components spanning a wide range of frequencies, vocal isolation from musical compositions, and the elimination of ambient crowd noise from live concert recordings. Also, one medical application that we considered was Blind Separation of Twin Fetal Heartbeats in an Electrocardiogram. The efficacy and quality of the estimated signals are scrutinized through the utilization of multiple quantitative metrics, thus enabling a comprehensive analysis. The research delves into three key areas: (1) BSS algorithms tailored for static models involving instantaneous mixtures, (2) the augmentation of BSS and Independent Component Analysis (ICA) through integration with sparseness or non-negativity constraints, and (3) BSS algorithms specifically designed for dynamic models entailing convolutive mixtures. Furthermore, the project explores three prominent methodologies: Independent Component Analysis (ICA), Non-Negative Matrix Factorization (NMF), and Neural Networks. This endeavor contributes to an in-depth understanding of the effectiveness and suitability of these approaches for Blind Source Separation, thereby facilitating advancements in audio signal processing.

Non-Contact Voltage Tester

We designed and implemented a Non-Contact Voltage Tester based on the principle of electromagnetic induction. This innovative device incorporates a mechanism that illuminates and triggers an audible alarm and an LED, to confirm the detection of voltage without requiring direct contact. The voltage tester provides a safe, efficient, and user-friendly solution for identifying the presence of electrical potential in a given area, which enhances safety measures and streamlines electrical troubleshooting processes in various applications, ensuring the well-being and operational efficiency of users.
